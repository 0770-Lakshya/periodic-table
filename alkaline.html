Alkaline earth metals are reactive elements found in Group 2 of the periodic table.

>>Alkaline earth metals are found in Group 2 of the periodic table. They are less reactive than alkali metals, harder, and have higher melting points. These elements have two electrons in their outer shell and form basic oxides and hydroxides.

  • Beryllium (Be)
  • Magnesium (Mg)
  • Calcium (Ca)
  • Strontium (Sr)
  • Barium (Ba)
  • Radium (Ra)
