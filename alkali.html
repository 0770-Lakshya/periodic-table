Alkali metals are highly reactive elements found in Group 1 of the periodic table.

>>Alkali metals are found in Group 1 of the periodic table. They are highly reactive, soft, silvery metals with low melting points. They react vigorously with water and are never found in their pure form in nature. They have only one electron in their outermost shell.

  • Lithium (Li)
  • Sodium (Na)
  • Potassium (K)
  • Rubidium (Rb)
  • Cesium (Cs)
  • Francium (Fr)
